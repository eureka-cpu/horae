The Horae design system

One editorial, low-light language across the whole suite — timesheet, approvals, reports, and invoicing. Warm-black surfaces, a grounded pine-and-brass palette, serif display, and mono numerals that keep every hour and total in rhythm.

Horae — goddesses of the hours and the seasons. Time, turned into paper.
01

Brand

Horae
The mark
A sundial at dawn — sun cresting a pine horizon, hour-ticks fanning above. It carries both ideas the Horae governed: the passage of hours and the turning seasons. Rays drop away below 24px; the rising sun still reads. The wordmark keeps its brass period.

Brand Identity · Dark · v1
Invoicer.
Time, turned into paper. An open-source time-tracking suite with billing that reads like a well-kept ledger.
Essence
01Editorial, not corporate. Serif display + mono numerals give it the calm of a printed statement.
02Low-light & precise. Warm-black surfaces for focus, luminous pine + brass for action — no orange, no generic indigo.
03Its own thing. Integrates with Harvest; never wears Harvest's skin.
The mark
Three rising bars = tracked hours becoming a total; the tall accent bar is the billable one. Reads on pine, ink, and brass.
